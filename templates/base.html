Sentiment Based Product Recommendation System

In this project, we have used User-User based Collaborative Filtering Recommendation system for custom recommendation of products for all users. Further, sentiment analysis is performed on reviews of those products recommended by the system and then top 5 best custom recommendations having high positive sentiments are displayed for each user. From the given list of users, please select one to view their custom product recommendations!
